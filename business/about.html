About Us

Regular grooming is essential to maintaining a healthy and happy life for your pup. We offer a full range of grooming services to help you ensure your pup gets all the care it needs

At Pretty Puppy, we understand your pup is more than just a pet, it's family, and we go the extra mile to ensure your dog is comfortable and happy.

All grooming services are performed in a cage-free environment because we want your dog's experience to be enjoyable.

Our expert stylists work patiently with your pup to provide a professional style with minimal stress. We take breaks often to cut down hip and joint fatigue from grooming services as well as mental stress that often comes with being away from home.
